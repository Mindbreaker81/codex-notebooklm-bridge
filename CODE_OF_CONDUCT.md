# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We expect participants to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## Expected Behavior

- Be courteous and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive data publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory conduct.
- Doxxing, threats, or attempts to expose personal information.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the repository maintainers through a private channel when available. If no private channel exists, open a minimal issue that requests a private contact method.
